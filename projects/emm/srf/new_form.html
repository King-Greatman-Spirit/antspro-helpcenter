  • Complete all mandatory fields (marked with *)
  • Specify exact equipment codes (e.g., BGT/PU/WSS-01)
  • Select appropriate priority level based on urgency
  • Include at least 3 potential suppliers when applicable
  • Attach supporting documents before submission
