Tabs Explained

Pipelines & Process Services (PPS) Nitrogen Projects Help Center: This section helps you manage all PPS projects from internal budgets to operational expenses. Track project status, view historical records, and monitor ongoing activities.

  1. Preparatory Projects: Focuses on planning, budgeting, and resource allocation for upcoming projects. Use this tab to lay the groundwork before execution begins.
  2. On-Going Projects: Track active projects currently in progress for better visibility and control. Stay updated with real-time changes and ensure timely decision-making.
  3. Completed Projects: Access archives of finished projects for reference and reporting. Useful for audits, performance reviews, and future planning.

Navigating and Managing Your Projects

To ensure a smooth experience managing your PPS Nitrogen-related projects, use the following tips and tools available on your dashboard:

  1. Search Input: Located at the top-right corner of your project table, this feature helps you quickly find specific projects by typing relevant keywords such as project name, project ID, department, or assigned team. Results update in real time as you type.
  2. Records Per Page Dropdown: Found at the top-left of the table, this dropdown lets you control how many projects are displayed at once. You can expand your view from 10 to 25, 50, or more projects per page for a broader overview.
  3. Pagination Controls: At the bottom of the table, you’ll see navigation buttons (1, 2, 3…) allowing you to move between pages. Use the “Previous” and “Next” buttons or click on a specific page number to browse through your project records easily.
  4. Sortable Columns: Click the small arrow icons next to each table header to sort projects by columns such as Start Date, Department, Project Manager, or Budget. Toggle between ascending and descending order for better visibility.

Pro Tip: Keeping project details updated regularly helps your team stay aligned and efficient.

Understanding the Table View

Each tab shows a table with the following key details to help you manage your PPS Nitrogen-related projects efficiently:

  1. Date: The date the project record was created or updated.
  2. Logo: Displays the client's logo for quick visual identification.
  3. Client: The organization or department the project is being executed for.
  4. Proj. Code: A unique identifier assigned to each project for easy reference.
  5. Project Title: A concise name or title representing the project’s purpose or scope.
  6. Type of Service: Specifies the category or nature of the service being rendered (e.g., Consultancy, Procurement, Maintenance).
  7. Client Rep.: The representative from the client’s side responsible for overseeing or approving the project.
  8. Job Status: Indicates the current progress or state of the project such as Pending, Ongoing, or Completed.
  9. View: A clickable button or link that redirects to the full project details for deeper insight.

Note: If you encounter any formatting issues or missing data, it may be due to an invalid entry. Kindly contact support for prompt resolution.

Pro Tip: Use the Search bar, Records per page dropdown, and column sorting to quickly filter and navigate through large sets of project data with ease.
