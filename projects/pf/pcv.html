PCV (Petty Cash Voucher) Management

This section helps you track and manage all Petty Cash Vouchers through the complete workflow - from issuance to archival with full audit trail.

Cash Disbursement Tracking

Navigating the PCV Dashboard

  • Path Tabs: Filter vouchers by workflow status (Issued, Pending Approval, etc.)
  • New PCV: Create new Petty Cash Voucher request.
  • Refresh: Update to see latest status changes.

PCV Workflow Paths

Vouchers progress through these distinct authorization paths:

  1. Issued & Archived: Completed vouchers that have been paid and filed for records.
  2. Pending Issue: Approved vouchers awaiting physical cash disbursement.
  3. Pending Approval: Requires authorization from designated approvers.
  4. Pending Submission: Draft vouchers being prepared before formal submission.

PCV Best Practices

  • Always include complete payee information
  • Record exact disbursement dates and times
  • Use to verify details before approval
  • Maintain sequential voucher numbering

Audit Requirements

  • All PCVs require dual signatures (preparer + approver)
  • Receipts must match voucher amounts exactly
  • No cash disbursements without approved PCV
