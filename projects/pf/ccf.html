About Cash Expense Claims

Cash Expense Claims are critical financial documents that:

  • Formally request reimbursement for business expenses
  • Document approved expenditures with proper receipts
  • Track department spending against project budgets
  • Create audit trails for financial accountability

Claims must be submitted within 14 working days after expense occurrence.

Workflow Status Guide

Current Status Overview

  • Issued & Archived: Completed claims
  • Pending Issue: Awaiting finance processing
  • Pending Approval: With department head
  • Pending Pass: With accounts verification
  • Pending Submission: Drafts not yet submitted

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Preparer: Submits complete claim with receipts
  • Verifier: Checks documentation accuracy
  • Approver: Authorizes payment
  • Processor: Issues payment
  • Archivist: Files completed claims

Cash Expense Claim Process Flow

Required Information:
  • Complete project details (NUN RIVER 7)
  • Accurate expense dates
  • Proper GL account coding
  • Detailed descriptions
  • Original receipts
Common Mistakes:
  • Missing supervisor approval
  • Illegible receipts
  • Incorrect project coding
  • Personal expenses included
  • Late submissions
Form Fields Explained:
Field Purpose Validation
Date Expense occurrence date Must be within current fiscal year
Form No Auto-generated claim ID Unique per claim
To Sign Approver designation Must be authorized signatory

Submission Checklist:
Submission Methods:
Online Portal (Preferred) Email to Accounts Physical Submission
Online submissions receive priority processing.

Standard Approval Path:
1
Department Head

Verifies business purpose

2
Accounts Verification

Checks documentation

3
Finance Approval

Authorizes payment

Approval Thresholds:
Amount Range Approver Level Timeframe
₦0 - ₦50,000 Supervisor 2 business days
₦50,001 - ₦200,000 Department Head 3 business days
₦200,001+ Finance Controller 5 business days
Emergency claims require additional MD approval regardless of amount.

Payment Methods:
Payment method affects processing time - bank transfers take 24-48 hours after approval.
Payment Status Indicators:
  • Pending Approval 1
  • Approved - Processing 2
  • Payment Issued 3
  • On Hold 4
  • Rejected 5

Completion Requirements:
  • Payment confirmation received
  • All documentation verified
  • No outstanding queries
  • Project code validated
  • Tax compliance confirmed
Completed claims are automatically archived in both digital and physical formats.
Retention Policy:
Document Type Retention Period
Original Claim Form 7 years
Supporting Receipts 7 years
Approval Records 10 years
Payment Evidence 10 years
Audit Access:

Archived claims can be retrieved for audit purposes by:

  1. Submitting a retrieval request to Finance
  2. Providing claim reference number
  3. Specifying audit purpose

Standard retrieval time is 2 business days.

NUN RIVER 7 Department FAQs

Track your claim through:

  1. Dashboard: Real-time status updates
  2. Email Notifications: Automatic workflow updates
  3. Finance Helpdesk: Call extension 4502
  4. Monthly Statements: Departmental expense reports
Claims exceeding standard processing times are automatically flagged for escalation.

Additional requirements for NUN RIVER 7 claims:

  • Project Code: Must include "NR7-" prefix
  • Approver: Requires dual sign-off for amounts >₦100,000
  • Documentation: Field operation claims need site manager verification
  • Coding: Special cost centers for river operations

Consult the NUN RIVER 7 Finance Manual for complete guidelines.

For emergency field claims:

  1. Mark as "URGENT - FIELD OPERATION" in subject
  2. Include satellite phone contact number
  3. Attach preliminary documentation
  4. CC the Field Operations Manager
Emergency claims still require full documentation within 72 hours of submission.
