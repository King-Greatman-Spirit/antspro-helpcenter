Financial Summary

System Alert

TOTAL OPEN EXPENSE: The total amount of expenses that are currently pending or unpaid (0.00).

OPEN MARGIN PROJECTED: The anticipated profit margin from all ongoing projects before final approval or closure (0.00).

OPEN MARGIN % PROJ.: The percentage representation of the projected margin based on current open items (0.00%).

PROJECT NOT QUALIFIED FOR AUTOMATIC FUNDING – This indicates that the current project does not meet the criteria for automatic financial support or release of funds.

Approval Workflow

COO/HOO Remarks

  • Notify: Choose who should be informed about this approval process (e.g., team members or stakeholders).
  • Manager's Remarks: Required comments or feedback from the manager regarding this submission.
  • Correspondence Remarks: Official communication or explanatory notes relevant to this request.

Approval Status

  • COO Approval: Status of Chief Operating Officer's review and decision – currently awaiting approval (Pending ✅).
  • Management Approval: Status of senior management's final review – currently pending confirmation (Pending ✅).
  • Authorised By: Name of the individual who submitted or confirmed the request (Greatman).
  • Submission Date: The exact date and time when the request was formally submitted (02/05/2025 13:24:23).
