ANTsPro II Asset Help Center

The Assets area helps you track and manage all company equipment and tools. It keeps records of what equipment exists, where it is, and its condition.

You Have Questions, We Have Answers

Key Asset Categories

Here are the different asset types you can manage:

  1. Equipment
    • Complete list of all company tools, machines, and physical assets.
    • Shows availability, location, and condition.
  2. GEMRS (General Equipment Movement Request Slips)
    • Tracks approval and movement of equipment between locations.
    • Required before transferring any asset.
  3. GRS (Equipment Return Slips)
    • Records when equipment is returned after use.
    • Includes condition checks.
  4. PHIN (Project Hardware Identification Numbers)
    • Tracks equipment assigned to specific projects.
    • Shows which assets are on active job sites.
  5. CRR (Condition Repair Reports)
    • Documents equipment damage and repair needs.
    • Used to request maintenance.
  6. SRF (Service Request Forms)
    • Formal requests for equipment maintenance/service.
    • Tracks approval and completion.
  7. MTCE (Maintenance Records)
    • Complete history of all equipment maintenance.
    • Shows service dates and technicians.
  8. MEMR (Monthly Equipment Maintenance Reviews)
    • Summary reports of equipment condition.
    • Used for preventive maintenance planning.
  9. STEM (Equipment Stems Report)
    • Tracks replaceable components and parts.
    • Shows usage life of critical pieces.
  10. Insp. Certs (Inspection Certificates)
    • Stores all equipment inspection documents.
    • Verifies assets meet safety standards.
  11. GEP (General Equipment Profile)
    • Complete technical specifications for each asset.
    • Includes manuals and operating instructions.
  12. D Logs (Daily Equipment Logs)
    • Tracks daily usage and operator checks.
    • Records hours of operation.
