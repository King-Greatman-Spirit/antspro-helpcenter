Asset / Equipment Registration Form

This form allows you to register and document new company assets or equipment with complete technical specifications and tracking information for proper inventory management.

You Have Questions, We Have Answers

Form Navigation

  • Dashboard: Returns to the main administrative overview.
  • Back: Navigates to the previous asset list view.
  • Save: Submits and stores the completed asset record.

Completing the Asset/Equipment Form

All required fields are marked with asterisks (*). Provide accurate information for proper asset tracking:

  1. Basic Information:
    • Date: Auto-populated with current date (editable if needed)
    • Category: Select from dropdown (e.g., Vehicles, Machinery, IT Equipment)
    • Type of Equipment: Specific classification of the asset
  2. Identification Details:
    • *BGT ID No.: Unique organizational tracking number
    • *Serial No.: Manufacturer's serial identifier
    • Registration No.: For vehicles or regulated equipment
    • Chassis No.: Vehicle frame identification
    • Engine No.: For engine-powered assets
  3. Manufacturer Specifications:
    • *Manufacturer: Company that produced the equipment
    • Make: Brand or product line
    • Model: Specific model designation
    • Colour: Physical appearance
  4. Dates & Valuation:
    • *Date of Manufacture: When item was produced
    • *Date of Purchase: Acquisition date
    • Equipment Cost: Purchase value in USD or NGN
    • *Depreciation %: Annual depreciation rate
  5. Additional Information:
    • Remarks: Special notes or conditions
    • Log: Auto-generated creation record showing:
      • Date of entry
      • User who created record
  6. Submission:
    • Click Save to permanently store the record
    • System validates required fields before acceptance
    • Successful submission generates a confirmation

Pro Tip: Complete all mandatory fields (marked with *) to ensure proper asset registration and tracking.

Accuracy Matters: Double-check serial numbers and identification details as they form the basis for all future tracking.
