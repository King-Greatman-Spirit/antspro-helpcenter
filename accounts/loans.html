ANTSPRO II (HR) Loans Help Center

The HR environment of ANTSPRO II is designed to streamline employee management processes, providing tools for tracking staff information, leave approvals, and department-specific details. This guide will help users navigate the HR dashboard effectively.

Navigating the HR Dashboard

Dashboard Features

  1. Top Navigation Bar:
    • Includes tabs such as Accounts , My Profile , Leave , Password , Back and Refresh.
    • Use these options to view accounts related information, update personal information, manage leave requests, reset passwords, go back or refresh the page.
  2. Second Navigation Bar:
    • This navigation bar contains additional options designed to provide quick access to essential HR and financial services:
      • Pay Slips – View and download your monthly or annual pay slips. Access secure, detailed records for financial documentation and verification.
      • Loans – Manage personal or company-backed loan information. View balances, repayment schedules, and submit loan adjustment requests.
      • Loan Apps. – Apply for new loans directly from the dashboard. Track application status and review documentation requirements.
      • Help – Access support resources, FAQs, and troubleshooting guides for resolving technical issues and understanding dashboard functionalities.
      • Support – Contact HR or IT support for assistance. Submit tickets, access live chat, and track the status of support requests.
    • These options are designed to enhance user experience by offering direct access to payroll information, loan applications, loan management, personal account settings, and support services, all from a single navigation bar.

Employee Accounts: Loans Management

The Employee Accounts: Loans Management interface allows employees to manage their loans and advances directly through the AntsPro Dashboard. Below is a breakdown of each section for better understanding:

Filter and Search Options

At the top of the page, there are options to filter and search loan records:

  • Show From: Allows you to specify a starting date for the records you want to view.
  • To: Defines the end date for the records displayed.
  • Search: Instantly filters the list based on date range, making it easier to locate specific transactions.

New Loan +

The New Loan + button allows you to initiate a new loan application. When clicked, it opens a form to input loan details, select repayment terms, and submit for approval. All new loan applications are tracked within the Loan Details section upon approval.

Loan Details

This section displays the details of any loans taken by the employee. Information includes:

  • Date: The date the loan was approved.
  • Amount: The total amount of the loan.
  • Deduction: The monthly deduction applied to repay the loan.
  • Approved by: The person who authorized the loan.
  • Ded. From: The date deductions start.
  • Ded. To: The date deductions are expected to end.
  • Remarks: Any additional notes or comments related to the loan.
  • Log: Historical changes or actions taken on the loan record.

Advance Details

The Advances section lists all salary advances taken. It includes:

  • Date: The date the advance was granted.
  • Amount Borrowed: The amount of money taken as an advance.
  • Monthly Deduction: The amount deducted monthly to repay the advance.
  • Approved by: The person who approved the advance.
  • Repayment Starts: The date repayments are scheduled to begin.
  • Repayment Ends: The expected end date for repayments.
  • Remarks: Additional notes or comments regarding the advance.
  • Log: History of changes and adjustments to the advance record.

Repayments - Deductions History

This section keeps track of all deductions made for loans and advances, including:

  • Date: The date of the repayment or deduction.
  • Approved Loan (Payroll): Deductions applied to payroll-based loans.
  • Cash Advance Ded.: Amounts deducted specifically for cash advances.
  • Loans Deductions: Total deductions made against active loans.
  • Remarks: Additional information or clarification on the deduction.
  • Log: Historical log of changes or updates to repayment records.
