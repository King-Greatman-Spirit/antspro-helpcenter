ANTSPRO II Human Resources (HR) Help Center

The HR environment of ANTSPRO II is designed to streamline employee management processes, providing tools for tracking staff information, leave approvals, and department-specific details. This guide will help users navigate the HR dashboard effectively.

Navigating the HR Dashboard

Dashboard Features

  1. Top Navigation Bar:
    • Includes tabs such as Accounts , My Profile , Leave , Password , Back and Refresh.
    • Use these options to view accounts related information, update personal information, manage leave requests, reset passwords, go back or refresh the page.
  2. Second Navigation Bar:
    • This navigation bar contains additional options designed to provide quick access to essential HR and financial services:
      • Pay Slips – View and download your monthly or annual pay slips. This section allows for secure access to your salary records for personal documentation and financial verification.
      • Loans – Manage information related to personal or company-backed loans. You can view loan balances, repayment schedules, and request loan adjustments if applicable.
      • Advances – Access information about salary advances or emergency funds requested through the company. This section includes details on disbursement dates and repayment terms.
      • Access – Manage your access permissions within the HR portal. This includes viewing roles, department-specific access, and submitting requests for access updates.
      • Password – Change or reset your account password for enhanced security. This section also includes password guidelines to maintain account safety.
      • Help – Access support resources, FAQs, and troubleshooting guides. This section is useful for resolving technical issues or understanding specific functionalities within the dashboard.
      • Support – Reach out to the HR or IT support team for personalized assistance. This includes submitting tickets for issues, live chat options, and viewing the status of previous support requests.
    • These options are designed to enhance user experience by offering direct access to payroll information, loan management, personal account settings, and support services, all from a single navigation bar.
  3. Side Menu:
    • Contains links to Home , Anniversary , Support , and Logout
    • These options facilitate quick navigation to general features and support resources.

Key Functionalities

The Personnel Bank & Pensions Information interface allows employees to securely view and manage their banking and pension details. This section is structured to ensure transparency and easy access to vital financial information.

Personnel Bank & Pensions Information

Overview

This page displays essential information related to an employee's bank and pension details, including:

  • Date: The date of record entry or last update.
  • Bank Branch: The specific branch where the account is maintained.
  • Bank: The name of the financial institution holding the account.
  • Bank Code: A unique identifier for the bank.
  • Sort Code: The code used to route money transfers.
  • Account Number: The employee's personal account number.
  • PFA Code: The Pension Fund Administrator (PFA) code associated with the account.
  • Name of PFA Manager: The manager assigned to oversee pension contributions.
  • RSA Number (PIN): The Retirement Savings Account (RSA) number for pension tracking.

How to Use This Page

  1. Review Your Information: Ensure all details are accurate. If discrepancies are found, contact HR or the Accounts Department.
  2. Update Requests: If changes are necessary, follow the company procedure for submitting banking or pension updates.
  3. Secure Your Information: Always log out after reviewing sensitive details to protect your personal information.

Tips for Efficient Use

  • Regularly verify your bank and pension information for accuracy.
  • Contact your PFA Manager directly for pension-related concerns.
  • For bank-related issues, refer to your bank branch for immediate support.
