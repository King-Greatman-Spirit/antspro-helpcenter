ANTSPRO II My Profile Official Information Help Center

The HR environment of ANTSPRO II is designed to streamline employee management processes, providing tools for tracking staff information, leave approvals, and department-specific details. This guide will help users navigate the HR dashboard effectively.

Navigating the HR Dashboard

Dashboard Features

  1. Top Navigation Bar:
    • Includes tabs such as Accounts , My Profile , Leave , Password , Back and Refresh.
    • Use these options to view accounts-related information, update personal details, manage leave requests, reset passwords, or refresh the page content.
  2. Side Menu:
    • Contains links to Home , Anniversary , Support , and Logout
    • These options provide quick access to support, system navigation, and logout features.

Key Functionalities

Profile Information Page

  • Overview: This page provides a comprehensive view of an individual staff member’s profile. It is categorized into tabs for Personal Info , Official Info , and Emergency details.
  • Profile Sidebar: Located on the left, this section includes the staff photo (avatar), full name, and role/title.
  • Information Tabs: The Official Info tab presents structured employment data, including:
    • Employee Number: Unique identifier assigned to each staff member.
    • Employment Date: The date the staff member officially joined the organization.
    • Employment Type: Specifies if the employee is full-time, part-time, contract, or temporary.
    • Department: The organizational unit where the staff member is assigned.
    • Position: The official job title or designation held by the employee.
    • Location: Geographical or office location where the staff member is based.
    • Leave Days Entitled: Number of annual leave days allocated to the employee.
    • Job Group: Classification level related to compensation and responsibilities.
    • Group Level: Specific level or rank within the job group, if applicable.
    • Confirmation Date: Date on which the employee’s status was formally confirmed.
  • Action Buttons: At the top of the details card:
    • Upload Photo – Add or change staff image
    • Update – Edit existing profile details
    • Exit Staff – Remove the user from active records
  • Navigation Controls: Use the top bar to move to other sections like:
    • Accounts – View all user records
    • Leave – Manage leave applications
    • Password – Reset credentials
    • Back or Refresh – Navigate or reload the page

Tips for Efficient Use

  • Regularly refresh the page using the Refresh button to ensure updated data visibility.
  • Use Ctrl + F (or Cmd + F on Mac) to search for specific staff information quickly.
  • Contact support via the provided link for technical issues or inquiries.
